title: "KOS "
---

**KOS (Kill On Sight) is <u>not allowed</u> on our server, in simple terms this means that you cannot <u>randomly kill people</u> with no reason.**

1. Shooting, Running Over, Punching or Meleeing people with **<u>0 reason/prior RP initiation</u>** is not permitted and you will be punished.
2. You can kill people who are inside your property after **<u>2 warnings</u>** if they are not leaving or making an attempt to leave.
3. You are allowed to **<u>KOS at envoys</u>** when someone is obviously going for an envoy, if someone is just going past because itâ€™s in a public area then do not KOS them.
4. You are allowed to KOS people who are partaking in the **<u>theft of a car</u>** (with the car heist plugin).
5. To kill a PD in a situation they must be **<u>making an attempt to arrest you</u>**. This is mainly just them asking for you to put your hands up. You **<u>must negotiate with PD if you have a hostage</u>**, you cannot KOS.
6. You are allowed to **<u>defend your teammates</u>** however to be defined as a teammate they must be in the **<u>same Steam or In-Game group</u>**. In the case if your teammate decides to break rules and KOS the other party, and they get shot back, you are **<u>not to help</u>** if you know they are breaking the rules.
7. If you are damaged in an RP situation then you are only permitted to **<u>take revenge during that RP situation</u>**, if the player escapes or is arrested etc then you may not attempt to kill them.
8. You can KOS anyone attempting to **<u>steal your car</u>**, they must either be **<u>lockpicking</u>** it or **<u>hotwiring</u>** it to KOS them!
